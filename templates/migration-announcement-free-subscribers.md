# Migration Announcement Email — Free Subscribers

Send this to your free subscribers around the time of the switch — either as one of your last sends from Substack, or as the first send from your new platform. Replace the `{{placeholders}}` with your platform's merge tags and adapt the "why" paragraph to your actual reasons.

Related: [paid subscriber version](migration-announcement-paid-subscribers.md) · [full checklist](../checklist.md#9-announce-the-migration)

---

**Subject:**

```txt
A small update about this newsletter
```

**Body:**

```md
Hi {{first_name}},

I’m moving this newsletter from Substack to a new publishing setup.

Nothing important changes for you. You will still receive the newsletter in your inbox, and you do not need to re-subscribe.

Why the move?

I want more control over the website, email experience, archive, analytics, and long-term direction of the publication.

The next issue will come from the new setup. To make sure it lands in your inbox, you can reply to this email or move the next issue to your primary inbox if it ends up somewhere weird.

Thanks for reading.

{{sender_name}}
```

---

## Tips

- Keep it short. Readers care about "do I need to do anything?" (no) far more than the platform details.
- The reply/move-to-inbox ask is doing real work — replies and inbox moves are strong deliverability signals for your new sending setup. Don't cut it.
- If your website URL is changing, add one line with the new address.
- If you have paid subscribers, do **not** rely on this email for them — send the [dedicated paid version](migration-announcement-paid-subscribers.md).
